Welcome to openQ Medical Relationship Management System 
The openQ system constitutes a dynamic database of information and utilities to help manage your company’s relationships with 
renowned medical Experts.
 
In addition to having the most current information about the Experts at your fingertips, you will be able to create development strategies; 
record interactions; schedule and track events; view their affiliations and much more.

This tool provides a fast and easy way for Business Unit Heads, Agency Representatives and other interest groups to manage and share information effectively.
